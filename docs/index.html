Currencies

A simple, ad-free currency converter for Android — built for travellers, not traders.

Google Play F-Droid Source

Why Currencies?

🔒

Privacy First

No ads, no analytics, no crash reporters. The only network call is fetching exchange rates from the provider you choose.

🌍

160+ Currencies

From major world currencies to exotic ones — choose from 6 exchange-rate providers offering 30–160+ currencies each.

🧮

Built-in Calculator

Do full arithmetic (+, −, ×, ÷) before converting. Perfect for splitting restaurant bills or calculating bulk prices.

📈

Historical Charts

See a full year of exchange-rate history at a glance, or look up rates on any past date back to 2010.

🎨

Material 3 Design

Clean adaptive UI with light, dark, and pure-black themes. Follows your system theme automatically.

📱

Foldable-Ready

Adaptive multi-pane layout on foldable devices. Proper support for hardware keyboards and all screen sizes.
