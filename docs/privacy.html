Privacy Policy

Short version: we collect nothing. The long version is below.

Last updated: July 2026

Summary

Currencies does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data. The app has no account system, no analytics, no advertising SDK, and no crash reporter. The sole network activity is fetching exchange rates from the provider you select.

Data Collected by This App

None. The app does not collect any data whatsoever from users.

Permissions

The app requests a single Android permission:

  • android.permission.INTERNET — required to download exchange rates from the selected provider. No other permission is requested or used.

The app does not request access to your location, contacts, camera, microphone, storage, phone state, or any other sensitive permission.

Network Activity

The app makes outbound HTTPS requests only to the exchange rate provider you have selected in Settings. The request contains:

  • The URL of the rates or history endpoint (publicly documented by each provider)
  • Your device's standard HTTP headers (User-Agent, Accept-Encoding, etc.)

No personally identifiable information is included in these requests. The providers' own privacy policies govern how they handle server-side access logs.

Provider Privacy Policies

  • Frankfurter.app — open-source proxy, no account required
  • OpenExchangerates — account required; subject to their privacy policy
  • InforEuro (European Commission)
  • Bank of Canada
  • Norges Bank
  • Bank Rossii

Local Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device in Android SharedPreferences. It is never transmitted to any server:

  • Cached exchange rates (from the last successful fetch)
  • Selected base and target currencies
  • Starred (favourite) currencies
  • User preferences (theme, fee percentage, provider choice, extended keypad toggle)
  • Last selected historical date

This data is cleared when you uninstall the app. It can also be cleared via Android Settings → Apps → Currencies → Clear Data.

Third-Party SDKs

Currencies does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or social-login SDK. The complete dependency list is public in the repository's app/build.gradle.kts file.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any data from children or from any user, regardless of age.

Changes to This Policy

If the privacy posture of the app ever changes materially, this page will be updated and the change will be noted in the release changelog.

Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, open an issue on the GitHub repository.
